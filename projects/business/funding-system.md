# Funding System

## Purpose
Create sustainable funding for Rays of Resilience ministry and community programs.

## Funding Sources

### Green Thumb Gardening Community
Online gardening education and product platform generating affiliate and product revenue.

### MRC Apparel
Clothing and merchandise brand supporting outreach and ministry work.

### Donations
Support from partners, donors, and community supporters helping fund ministry programs.

## Use of Funds

• Orphanage support  
• Food and daily needs  
• Child education assistance  
• Church development  
• Community outreach programs

## Long Term Goal
Build stable funding systems so ministry and community programs can grow without relying only on donations.
